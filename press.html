Nordic Startup Bits: Nominated for Nordic Startup Awards

The founders of Aquasolis Global aim to provide the whole world with clean water - and get rid of plastic litter on the road.

Will give the whole world water. The invention to Øyvind (22), Ole (23) and Nitesh (22) can save millions from the water crisis and reduce both plastic and greenhouse gas emissions at the same time

The company Aquasolis Global is working on water purification and is part of The Explorer.

The Norwegian company Aquasolis was nominated in the category 'Rising Star'.
